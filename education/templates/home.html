Our Aims & Objectives

Why do we study English?

English is a global language. If you are good at it, you can get yourself into schools abroad, you can have market competent jobs, you can have access to the resources on the internet, you can travel internationally with no hesitation. It is the top language of the internet, so if you know English you can be a part of the social world, you can have friends over the internet, you can read books by English authors and you can also watch Hollywood blockbusters. English not only open doors to new career opportunities but also it will give you access to unlimited entertainment.

What is yousaidhi?

It is an ESL learning website designed to help foreign students practice and improve their English speaking and learning ability. It is unique in the sense that it always offers quality classes at a very affordable rate. You can book a lesson anytime 24/7 with your teacher. You can choose courses from a wide range of lessons as per your level, be it beginner, intermediate or advanced. We’ve got it all covered

Why yousaidhi?

Here, our primary goal is to provide our students comfort, confidence, and consistent room for improvement. We have competent teachers from all over the world who have gained certificates from reputable institutes. Our lessons are designed by experts who are native English speakers. Learning a new language can be a little tiring, but at yousaidhi, we can guarantee all your classes will be fun and productive class.

What can you expect from yousaidhi?

All our teachers hold a minimum degree of bachelor’s or above, are experienced and internationally certified to teach foreign students. They are selected carefully and undergo rigorous training so that they can give out the best to their students. Each lesson is tailored to fit 25 minutes which ensures maximum output within minimum time.
